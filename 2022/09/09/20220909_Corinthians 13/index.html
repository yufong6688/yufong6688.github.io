Corinthians 13

From The Bible

  1. If I speak in the tongues of men and of angels, but have not love, I am only a resounding gong or a clanging cymbal.

  2. If I have the gift of prophecy and can fathom all mysteries and all knowledge, and if I have a faith that can move mountains, but have not love, I am nothing.

  3. If I give all I possess to the poor and surrender my body to the flames, but have not love, I gain nothing.

  4. Love is patient, love is kind.
    It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud.

  5. It is not rude, it is not self-seeking, It is not easily angered; it keeps no record of wrongs

  6. Love does not delight in evil but rejoices with the truth.

  7. It always protects, always trusts,
    always hopes, always perseveres.

  8. Love never fails.
    But where there are prophecies, they will cease; where there are tongues, they will be stilled; where there is knowledge, it will pass away.

  9. For we know in part and we prophesy in part,

  10. but when perfection comes, the imperfect disappears.

  11. When I was a child, I talked like a child, I thought like a child, I reasoned like a child. When 1 became a man, I put childish ways behinc me.

  12. Now we see but a poor reflection as in a mirror; then we shall see face to face.
    Now I know in part; then I shall know fully, even as I am fully known.

  13. And now these three remain: faith, hope and love. But the greatest of these is love.

方法一

簡單只要一行程式,缺點按鈕按很多下會一起發音,新增音檔段落超花時間,一段音檔要改三個地方
優點就是很像文字點讀,可直接點在字上面,但是因為是按鈕形式,所以沒辦法選取按鈕裡的文字,
就算瀏覽器支援選字翻讀,可惜選不到,沒辦法針對某個單字選字翻譯

方法二的主程式

方法三簡化播放符號

集前面程式之優點,最大的優點是只要新增音檔時,加上mp3檔就可以了,不用去修改更多參數,
而且不同於方法一,這個可以直接選到字,所以萬一某些字不會唸或不懂字意,這個方法可以選字翻讀
很方便,唯一最大的缺點就是按鈕超醜的,如果按鈕能縮小改成淡色的就好了
